Why Austin?

Austin, the Live Music Capital of the World

Texas's state capital has kept its small-town heart, earning the love with great music, culinary prowess, whip-smart locals and a sociable streak impossible to resist.

Unique trips are an Austin speciality. From stand-up paddle boarding and music venues for every taste to eclectic restaurants and vintage shopping finds, there's plenty to do in this incredible city.

Restaurants

My favourite restarants in Austin

Terry Black's BBQ

Spacious, casual stop for pit-smoked meats & housemade sides from famous 4th-generation pitmasters.

Address:

1003 Barton Springs Rd

Why you should visit

Texas is famous for its great BBQ food and Terry Black's BBQ is no exception. This was the best BBQ I ate in Texas, but be prepared to queue for it!

Loro

Asian smokehouse meets Texas barbecue in a rustic spot with beer, wine, and cocktails, plus a giant patio.

Address:

2115 S Lamar Blvd

Why you should visit

An incredible Asian-BBQ fusion restaurant where everything on the menu looks incredible. Their ramen bowls are delicious!

Juiceland

Health-minded chain serving juices, smoothies & hot drinks, plus noodle bowls & salads.

Address:

1625 Barton Springs Rd

Why you should visit

The best smoothies I've ever tried! The Honey Beary is great for a morning treat, but their Smoothie Bowls and Juices are also amazing.
